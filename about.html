Our Story

Built on discipline, not on hustle.

OrlaDent exists because the industry kept asking labs and clinics to choose between speed and standards. We built a design house that refuses the trade.

Company Story

The relentless search for "exact."

OrlaDent was founded on a simple observation: the CAD/CAM outsourcing market had split into two broken extremes — corporate platforms running AI-scaled ticket queues, and freelance marketplaces with inconsistent, unverifiable quality. Neither one earned trust; both simply asked for it.

We built the opposite. A design house where turnaround is scoped case by case instead of promised in one flat number, where every client speaks to a real, named designer instead of a queue, and where "premium" is proven on the very first case — free — rather than claimed in a sales deck.

Our crest, the seated queen and harp, is a quiet nod to that standard: sovereignty earned through discipline, not declared by title. It's why our line is Exactness, Earned — nothing here is assumed.

Brand Philosophy

Speed is not a discount signal — it's evidence of technical mastery. We claim "fast," "precise," and "premium" at once, because discipline is what makes those claims compatible.

Mission

To give every dental lab and clinic overflow capacity, boutique-level margins, and chairside-speed turnaround — without ever asking them to gamble on quality to get it.

Vision

To be the design partner labs and clinics call before they ever have to split a case across multiple outsourcers — a single, trusted bench extension, coast to coast.

Core Values

Three Laws, one standard.

Internally, we call these the Three Laws — the mythology behind the mark. Externally, they're just how every case is measured.

Law I · Exactness

Precision Under Pressure

Flawless margins and perfect occlusion on every design — the proof that makes our speed credible instead of suspicious.

Law II · The Royal Court

The Human Line

A direct, human relationship with every partner — never a faceless messenger, marketplace, or ticket queue.

Law III · The Vault

Earned Gold

Premium demonstrated case by case against real, published turnaround data — never a blanket promise.

Brand Personality

Confident. Precise. Low-hype.

No "hustle" language, no urgency theater. Turnaround speed is framed as evidence of control and discipline — never as a rush. Gold is used the way we talk: as an earned accent against black, never gaudy.
